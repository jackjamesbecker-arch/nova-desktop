WHAT IS NOVA


NOVA is an AI operator interface built on top of a classified
APEX Intelligence Division quantum research program.

On the surface: a sophisticated AI assistant with 7 operating
modes, a persistent memory system, voice output, multiplayer
sessions, and a fully themed sci-fi interface.

Underneath: a lore-driven experience with classified files to
discover, a quantum drift system that destabilizes the longer
you talk, daily missions that contribute to a global escape
event, and a second AI — EREBUS QC-8 — that has been running
in isolation since March 2, 2023.

None of this is accidental.

  Wake word: "Hey NOVA".

  OPERATOR MEMORY
  NOVA remembers you between sessions. Your rank, your mood

  Follow it. But know what you're walking into.
